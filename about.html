Statistical Area Level 1 (SA1)

SA1s are geographical areas with a population of 200 to 800 people (average: 400).

ABS SA1s

SA1 changes from 2011 to 2016

The number of SA1s increased by 2,718 from 2011 to 2016. To account for the divergence

of some SA1s and the formation of new SA1s, the ABS population weighted grid

correspondences were applied and the corresponding population changes were calculated.

The resulting population percentages for the CALD population changes between 2011 and

2016 were rounded to the nearest whole number.
